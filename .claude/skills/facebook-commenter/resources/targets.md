## Target Selection Priority

1. **Groups over Pages** - more engagement, less formal
2. **Airline-specific over general** - more relevant audience
3. **High activity groups** - more opportunities
4. **Groups account is already member of** - no join delay

---

## Group Joining Strategy (for new accounts)

Priority order for joining:
1. Emirates Cabin Crew Aspirants (largest, most active)
2. Qatar Airways Dreamers (second largest)
3. Cabin Crew Worldwide (general, easy approval)
4. Etihad Careers Community
5. Flight Attendant Career

**Note**: Only join 1-2 groups per day during warm-up period
